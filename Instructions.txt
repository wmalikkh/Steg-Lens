📦 StegLens 0.2 – Installation & Usage Instructions
🛠️ How to Use:
Download and extract the ZIP file.

Open the extracted StegLens-0.2 folder.

Navigate to the build folder inside.

Double-click StegLens.exe to launch the application.

Wait a few seconds – your web browser will automatically open it.

✅ No installation required.
✅ Works 100% offline on your local machine.
✅ Secure encryption, hiding, and extraction.
